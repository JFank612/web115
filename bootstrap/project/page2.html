Before you jump into any dieting and exercising, you must learn about the different types of diets and variations of exercising that will help benefit you the most. The first thing you need to do, is find out and determine any restrictions you might have that would prevent you from certain diets/exercises. If you are unsure of any, you can talk to your parents if they know of any history in your famliy or meet with a doctor to help find out. Once you have determined any restrictions, then you can emlinate any specific diets/exercises for yourself. The next thing to do is to do research on diets and exercises that will fit your regimen. There are a few different ways you can go about doing that. Probably the most popular way is to use the internet for your research. Another way you could do your resaerch is to go to your local library and read up about them or lastly you could make an appointment with a doctor or some other type of health consultant.
