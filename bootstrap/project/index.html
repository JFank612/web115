Living a healthy lifestyle

In life, it is known that if you want to live a longer life, you must practice a health lifestyle. The problem with the perfect health lifestyle that everyone wishes to live, is that it is not the easiest thing to maintain. For some people, its just not finding the right balance in your nutritional intake, while others have issues with the physical exercises needed. Also, another problem some people face, is their family issues and conditions that could restrict them from certain things in their lifestyles.
