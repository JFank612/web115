Putting it all together

Along with the diet and exercises, there other factors that will also help you live healthy. Sleeping and drinking plenty of water are other major keys in helping your metabolism. Also posture is a small factor with the way you sit, could hurt your back or other areas, and prevent you from being able to exercise. Now that you have figured out your diet and your exercise, its time to put it together and start or continue your healthy living.
