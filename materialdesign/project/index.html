Hello. Nice to meet you again.

Here at Deja Brew, we are about two things, fun mysteries and good brew. The idea of the brewery started early for me when I was young and was very fascinated with the supernatural. I was always diving deep into books about all the different types of myths of the world, and had dreamt of all the interesting things out there, that no one has every truly witnessed. As I got older, I realized there was no true profession out there for these types of things, but still made it a hobby to research everything I could. Once I was at that age, I started to drink a little bit to compliment my research but I noticed there were very few selections that fit my style. Thats when I started to dabble in the field of brewing. I had much fun between my newly found hobby and my other one so much, that I had the idea to combine the two together. Luck for me, I was able to test my new concoctions with fellow friends of mine, and they seemed to enjoy it so much, they encouraged me to start my own line of brews, and thats when Deja Brew was born.
