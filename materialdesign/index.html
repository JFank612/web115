BeerFest

Are you 21 years of Age?

Here on May 4, we will be hosting a beerfest for all you craft beer lovers. The event is located in the park and will run from 3pm till 7:30pm. We will have many different breweries with many different types of their beer here with them.

There will be a limited amount of tickets sold, so you will want to buy your tickets ASAP. We have three different types of tickets being sold. Our first set of tickets will the General Admission tickets for those who just to come and enjoy the brew. The next set of tickets will be the VIP deal which gets you in one-hour earlier and will get you first in line for the early tappings of the beer available. The final set of tickets will be for the designated drivers, which will give you a red armband that indicates you will the designated driver for the event and will limit you to only two beverages.

For anyone that will be attending the event, you must have a ride set up for you outside of it. There will be police setup outside of the event monitoring everyone leaving.

For tickets, enter number needed and you will be redirected to the checkout webpage
  • General Admission - $50
  • VIP - $75
  • Designated Driver - $25

Movie in the Park

New tradition

Starting the first weekend of May, we will be presenting a movie in the park at 5:30pm, and will run till the end of the summer on every Sunday. Every movie presented will be a family-friendly movie for everyone to enjoy. For our first movie ever, we will be showing the movie Shrek. After this first weeek we will be having a vote on the choice of movie then. We hope to have a good turn out for these showing and that everyone enjoys it!

Petting Zoo

Mr. Jones Travelling Petting Zoo

On Wednesday, May 9th, we will be hosting a petting zoo for all the kids. It will be set up at the fairgrounds and will go from noon to 3pm. There will be many different pens for all the different animals, The list on animals will consist of:

  • Pigs
  • Goats
  • Llamas
  • Chickens and roosters
